DB-303

Mutant Diode Bass

If you love hearing a 303 getting punished to its limits, this is for you! Circuit-modelled diode ladder, screaming nonlinearities, and more:

  • Multimode morphing filters
  • DevilFish mods
  • Step-sequencer with palm-mute & hammer-ons
Try DB-303 Free

KlangwerX

Drum Synth for WAV Export

Inspired by 90s legends like Stomper and JOMOX. Craft punchy kicks and percussive sounds, then export to WAV for your productions.

  • Analog-style drum synthesis
  • One-shot WAV export
  • 90s techno/house sound design
Try KlangwerX

Funky Monk

The famous VST, reincarnated!

A polyphonic formant with XY vowel control for freaky vocal fun.

  • Classic formant synthesis with extra controls
  • Trance Gate
  • mono/poly up to 8 voices
Try FunkyMonk

BAWD

Binaural Beat Generator

Tune your brain with binaural beats. Delta for deep sleep, theta for meditation, alpha for relaxation, beta for focus.

  • Binaural frequency control (0-20 Hz beat)
  • Pink noise + amplitude modulation
  • Sine/square waveform morphing
Try BAWD

About

Löwen Labs is the audio research project of David Lowenfels, a full-stack developer and DSP engineer based in Northumberland, UK.

With 15+ years of experience spanning Ruby on Rails consulting, synthesizer development, and signal processing research, David brings a unique blend of web technology and audio expertise to browser-based instruments.

Notable past projects include DSP work on the TimeWARP 2600 (used by Depeche Mode), the Pranawave HRV biofeedback system, and contributions to DB-303 based on midilab's Open303 engine.
